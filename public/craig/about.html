My Practice Philosophy

I believe in providing my patients with the best possible foot care. I am well-trained in traditional foot surgery, modern minimal-incision surgery, and the lastest technologically-advanced LASER surgery.

Podiatry has come a long way from trimming corns and filing nails. The underlying causes of corns, callouses, and ingrown nails are now understood and treatable. Rather than repeated trimming and filing, I advocate treating the underlying cause of the problem. I outline the surgical options: traditional, minimal-incision, or LASER therapy. Then taking into account each individual patient's needs and desires, we decide together on a specific treatment plan. I strive to educate each patient about his/her particular foot problem, so he/she can fully participate in chooding a treatment plan.

Most procedures are done in the comfortable setting of my office. Usually my patients can walk immediately after the procedure. The healing time is usually very brief. I see my post-surgical patients frequently to keep a close eye on the healing process to make keep on the right path for a full recovery. Because I am very concerned about my patient's comfort, I usually prescribe a few pain pills for the evening after surgery. Many patients tell me they did not nee to take a single pill because the discomfort was so minimal, but I would rather keep it on the safe side and allow my patients to enjoy the most comfort possible after surgery.

I welcome questions from all of my patients, and I am always happy to discuss each individual foot problem. I want to work with you to resolve your foot needs. Please don't hesitate to call me.

Experience

Since graduation from podiatry school, I have helped more than 50,000 patients with foot problems. I have take care of thousands of warts, ingrown nails, bunions, and hammertoes. I am very experienced in LASER therapy of soft tissue problems: fungal nails, ingrown nails, neuromas, scars, and warts. I own a Hi-Tech-Laser and have taught other doctors and podiatrists the technique of LASER surgery.
